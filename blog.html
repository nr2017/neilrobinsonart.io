This blog is based around my printmaking project. My work depict the diverse architectural styles of my hometown and the surrounding landmarks new and old. I like to experiment with different types of print making from screenprinting to chine colle. In time, I hope to produce a book and ultimately hold an exhibition of my prints at the town’s art gallery at some point in the near future. I have already produced a number of prints related to Stockport and it’s past. You can view my work on the Print Gallery page of this website. Please visit my blog Stockport In Print. If you have a Facebook or WordPress account, you can subscribe to my latest posts.
